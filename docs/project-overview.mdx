# iComplain - PROJECT OVERVIEW

## PROJECT INFORMATION

**Institution:** University of Saint La Salle - Bacolod
**Target User:** Students, Administrative Staff
**Project Type:** Web-based Facility Issue Tracking System

---

## PROBLEM STATEMENT

### Nature of the Problem

Educational institutions have the responsibility to maintain facilities and responsive systems in supporting the effectiveness of learning.
Classrooms, restrrooms, air coniditioning, lighting, internet, and other equipment need to be in their tip-top shape for it to be of better service to the students.

**Current Issues:**
- Problems with facilities are unavoidable and will eventually be encountered
- Issues are reported, but there is **no centralized system** for observing progress and keeping complaints
- Reported problems are not resolved efficiently
- Unresolved facility issues hinder the effectiveness of learning environments
- Student satisfaction is affected, raising concerns about accountabiilit and institutional efficiency

### Who & Where

- **Affected Users:** Students at USLS - Bacolod
- **Location:** University of St. La Salle - Bacolod Campus
- **Scope:** All institutional facilities (classrooms, restrooms, AC, lighting, internet, equipment, and etc.)

---

## Benefits of Solving the Problem

### For Students

- Consistently well-maintained learning environment supporting educational success
- Faster response times to reported issues minimizing disruptions
- Transparency in the resolution process building confidence
- Clear visibility that concerns are being addressed

### For the Institution

**Accountability & Efficiency:**
- Clear ownership of each reported issue
- Measurable response time metrics
- Comprehensive data on facility performance
- Better resource allocation based on urgency and impact
- Historical data informing strategic decisions about equipment replacement, renovation priorities, and preventive maintenance scheduling

**Improved Communication:**
- Clear channel for students and faculty to report issues and check status
- Complete visibility for maintenance teams into all pending work
- Administrative reports demonstrating institutional responsiveness
- Identification of areas needing additional investment
- Transparency building trust within the campus community
